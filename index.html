Wim Hof, also known as The Iceman, is a Dutch extreme athlete noted for his ability to withstand freezing temperatures.

Here is the timeline of Ice Man's life...

  • 1959 - Sittard, Limburg, Netherlands

  • 2000 - Sittard, Limburg, Netherlands

  • 2007 - , Hof set a world record for fastest half marathon barefoot on ice and snow

  • 2007 - Hof climbed to an altitude of 7,200 metres (23,600 ft) on Mount Everest wearing nothing but shorts and shoes

  • 2009 - Hof reached the top of Mount Kilimanjaro within two days wearing only shorts and shoes

  • 2016 - He reached Gilmans point on Kilimanjaro with journalist Scott Carney in 28 hours
